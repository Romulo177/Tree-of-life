Introduction

This website was developed for the course T03-CVI-302 - Genetic Information Decoding, taught by Dr. Juliana Helena Costa Smetana.

Its purpose is to organize all course topics in a didactic and structured way, using the metaphor of a tree to represent the relationships between concepts in biology and genetics.

The content is divided into four conceptual levels:

  • Root Concepts – Fundamental biological principles
  • Trunk Concepts – Core cellular and genetic processes
  • Branch Concepts – Specialized systems and mechanisms
  • Leaf Concepts – Applications, modern tools, and real-world impacts

Each concept is also tagged to indicate whether it applies to eukaryotic cells , prokaryotic cells, or to both.
